Physics

  • simulation of conservation of momentum

    Interactive tool to visualize the change in velocity when two masses collide.

  • simulation of light refraction

    Here you can see how light changes it's direction when entering another medium. For now only the refraction for air-to-water is available.

  • speed of pendulum mass

    This simulation shows the influence of mass, length and starting angle on the pendulum's speed. The current speed is shown in a plot.

  • simulation of two connected masses

    This simulation will show you how big a mass has to be to balance out a second mass. Both masses are connected via a rope running over a pulley. The first lies on a slope while the second one hangs down othe other edge.
